    Ingredients For Poloze Verde!

  • Bone In Chicken (thighs, breast, legs… whatever you want ! just needs to be bone in)
  • Cilantro
  • Tomatillos
  • Bell Pepper
  • Serrano Chili
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Chicken Bouillon
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder
  • Black Pepper
  • Hominy
  • Lemon

    Step by Step!

  1. Prep your veggies! Wash, chop, and peel all veggies.
  2. Add your bone in chicken, spices(chicken bouillon, garlic powder, onion powder,black pepper, & salt), chopped onions, bell pepper, & garlic into a large pot with water. Bring it to a rolling boil.
  3. Allow this broth to cook for 15 minutes. While it is boiling, find another pot and throw your tomatillos into it and boil again. These will be done when the tomatillos roll over! Try not to let them overcook in split.
  4. If you are a good host, once the chicken is boiled you can shred it. I don’t! I never said I am a good host.
  5. Fish out all the veggies from your chicken broth and place into a blender. Add your tomatillos as well. This is also the time to add extra garlic, your serrano peppers and cilantro.
  6. Blend! Add chicken broth to it and the skin for smoothness.
  7. Add the mixture to the chicken broth. Add back in your possibly shredded chicken.
  8. Taste for flavor! Add any spices you think it needs.
  9. Allow the soup to boil for 20 mins.
  10. During this time prep your toppings! Chop limes, onions, cilantro. Whatever you want.
  11. When it is almost 20 minutes add in your hominy. Let it boil to your desired softness.
  12. Add toppings and eat!
